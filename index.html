About

If you've ever used PowerPoint Copilot to clean up a messy slide, there’s a small chance you've run into some of my work[cite: 1]. I was involved as a Data Scientist on that team at Microsoft[cite: 1]. Before that, I completed my undergrad at IIT Gandhinagar[cite: 1].

During my time there, I got to work on some exciting research[cite: 1]. My work focused on vision-language models[cite: 1], diffusion-based generative AI[cite: 1], and few-shot medical image classification[cite: 1]. Through research internships at IISc Bangalore, the University of Bath, and IIIT Hyderabad, I gained experience with diffusion transformers, attention-based image editing, and anomaly detection in autonomous driving[cite: 1].

But right now, I’m taking a big step that I have been incredibly eager to start: my PhD at Johns Hopkins University, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Vishal Patel[cite: 1]. I'm genuinely excited to jump into research and see where this next chapter takes me.

Thanks for stopping by! Feel free to look around or reach out if you'd like to say hi.
